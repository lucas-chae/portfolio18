PLAN A

Chat, Plan, Explore.

PLAN A is a travel planning app that will help people plan their vacation personalized to their needs. It was my first time being part of a development team and giving life to a functional prototype. I led interviews for different user archetypes to identify problems, generated wireframes and prototypes, and conducted field studies to finalize the designs. Why don't people use planners?
TL;DR

"Customization" doesn't really work well. Unnecessary and poorly designed features make it too complicated to use.

Although trip planning appls have been experimented with many times in the past, they haven’t really stuck widely and they’ve mostly been focused on aggregating information rather than making trip planning a more enjoyable experience. Instead, we want to focus on thinking of new user interface elements and layouts for easy creation and modification, and clear summarization of trips. Existing solutions aren’t helpful enough and are largely just lists of information with very generic trips for general audiences. They don’t really allow for a good way to adjust the plan and don’t adapt to users. Considering the unpopularity of most travel planning applications or websites, out of the people that we know and our own experiences, it seems like most of the time people just search on Google or look through huge lists of most popular locations on websites like TripAdvisor in order to plan their trip because they don’t trust that the final result will match something that they wanted.
